# Webcam Overlay

Include your face in recordings with a floating camera feed.

## Settings

| Setting | Value |
|---------|-------|
| Position | Top-right corner |
| Size | 200x200px |
| Camera | Front-facing (wide-angle) |
| Mirroring | Enabled |
| Corner Radius | 32pt |

## How to Enable

1. Open [Settings > Recording](/docs/settings)
2. Enable "Show Webcam Overlay"
3. Grant camera permission when prompted

## Use Cases

- **Tutorials** - Show your face while demonstrating
- **Presentations** - Add a personal touch to screen recordings
- **Support videos** - Connect with viewers while troubleshooting
- **Educational content** - Engage students during lessons

## Tips

- The webcam overlay appears during recordings only
- Position is fixed to top-right to avoid important content
- Mirroring makes it natural to look at while recording
